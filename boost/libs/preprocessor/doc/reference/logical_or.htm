#include <boost/preprocessor/logical/or.hpp>

#define BOOST_PP_OR(X,Y)

Expands to the logical OR of the operands.

For example, BOOST_PP_OR(0,2) expands to 1 (a single token).

#define BOOST_PREPROCESSOR_OR(X,Y)
