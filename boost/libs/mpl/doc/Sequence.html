Sequence

Description

A Sequence is any compile-time entity to that you can apply begin?/end? operations in order to get iterators for accessing the range of its elements.

Expression semantics

 Expression  Requirement  Semantics  
begin<a>::typeA model of TrivialIterator
end<a>::typeA model of TrivialIterator
