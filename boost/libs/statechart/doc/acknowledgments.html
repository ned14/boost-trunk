The boost::fsm library

Acknowledgments
Very special thanks go to Aleksey Gurtovoy, the developer of the ingenious meta programming library (boost::mpl). The interface as well as the implementation of boost::fsm hugely benefit from Alekseys work. I would have given up long ago without mpl. Moreover, Aleksey's double dispatch implementation in his FSM framework gave me fresh ideas after I had come to the conclusion that my dynamic_cast-based solution was too bloody bad ;-).

Special thanks go to:

  • Peter Dimov for developing two other important libraries (lightweight_mutex, intrusive_ptr) boost::fsm is building on.
  • the countless folks who developed important boost infrastructure like config, regression tests, build, html templates, etc.

I would also like to thank:

  • David Abrahams and Gustavo Guerra for giving feedback on a very early (and clumsy) design of this library.
  • Aleksey Gurtovoy, Douglas Gregor and Jeff Garland for their encouragement to continue working on this library.
